Northy is a designer based in shanghai. The desktop version of this portfolio site is preferred to be interact with. For info or inquiry please email

Northy is an incurable romantic who believes that everyday objects around us also have feelings and “OBJECTALITY”, just like human beings. She builds up imaginary scenarios for everything around her: a HAMSTER, a SOFA, a WINE BOTTLE & a GLASS, and so on.

She once found a discarded massage chair, took it apart and reupholstered it. Later, she shared the story plot she came up for the chair and collected 86 varations from NARRATORS she picked up on the streets.

She currently works at WIEDEN+KENNEDY, where she groups with beautiful people to hack TYPEWRITERS to print portraits,
or ships FEDEX BOXES filled with Shanghai goodies to other Wieden offices. Sometimes, she still has to jump into the role as an art director to create works for clients like NIKE, TIFFANY, MICHELIN, etc. Before she came to shanghai, she worked with crazy talented people at LITTLEBITS in new york to attack MoMA stores, making up some of the best times of her life.

She attended RISD to study Industrial Design. She partnered with her studio mate to design a CODESIGN TOOLBOX for non-designers, and powered up an umbrella to literally rain CATS&DOGS. She also designed electric sockets called DAY-OFF that switch off power by turn, spent some quality time at a wood shop to carve an INCENSE VESSEL, and decorated her studio wall with the LIGHTSCAPE.
