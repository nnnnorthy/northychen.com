Northy is an incurable romantic who believes that everyday objects around us also have feelings and “OBJECTALITY”, just like human beings. She builds up imaginary scenarios for everything around her: A HAMSTER, A DISCARDED MASSAGE CHAIR, A WINE BOTTLE & A GLASS, and so on.

She currently works at Wieden + Kennedy, where she groups with beautiful people to hack TYPEWRITERS to print portraits, or ships FEDEX BOXES filled with random goodies around the world. Sometimes, she still has to jump into the role as an art director to create works for clients like NIKE, TIFFANY, MICHELIN, etc. Before she came to Shanghai, she worked with crazy talented people at LITTLEBITS in New York to attack MoMA stores, making up some of the best times of her life.
