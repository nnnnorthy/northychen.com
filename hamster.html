HAMSTER MAZE
2013

This was a 3-weekend-project which started with an email from Emily. She told me littleBits was going to launch the logicBit bundle, and asked if I would be interested to come up with a project to feature the new product. I got to win some free littleBits. I said yes, and decided to use those logicBits to confuse my pet.

I designed a maze for Katyusha, a cute hamster lived with me in Shanghai. She was very smart, a bit aggressive, but a quite adorable young lady in general. She did some test runs with the maze before I embedded the circuits. A decent amount of sunflower seeds definitely helped the situation.

The maze included several vibrating obstacles, a rotating stage with optical illusion pattern, multiple lighting signs with human language, and a non-stop hamster running machine

For the complete instructions and more info about the awesome logicBits, you should visit my littleBits invention page.
