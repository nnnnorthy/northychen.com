HAMSTER MAZE
2013

This is a 3-weekend-project which starts with an email from Emily. She told me littleBits was going to launch the logicBit bundle, and if I would be interested to come up with a project to feature the new product, I got to win some free bits. So I said yes and decided to use those logicBits to confuse my pet.

I designed a maze for Katyusha, who is a cute hamster lives with me in Shanghai. She's very smart, a bit aggressive, but a quite adorable young lady in general. She did some test runs with the maze before I embedded the littleBits. A decent amount of sunflower seeds definitely helps the situation.

The maze includes several vibrating obstacles, a rotating stage with optical illusion pattern, multiple lighting signs with human language, and a non-stop hamster running machine.

For the complete instructions and more info about the awesome logicBits, you should visit my littleBits invention page.
