MICHELIN "DREAM DRIVING" CAMPAIGN
2013

As a brand, Michelin is not only selling tyres, but providing car lovers exceptional driving experiences.

The “Dream Driving “ digital campaign, which aims to engage Michelin’s premium audiences, rolls out across Weibo and key mobile platforms to inspire consumers to explore the ultimate driving experience and share their dream driving route.

We invite social influencers to share their driving experience in extreme profession or daily life to inspire the community. Also, we design a series of questionnaire allows Michelin fans to explore their own extraordinary driving experiences across a number of landscapes, including Tibet, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Xi’an, Yunnan and Chengdu. Two lucky winners who garner the most support via social media will be equipped to hit the road and live their “Dream Driving” experience.
