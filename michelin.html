MICHELIN "DREAM DRIVING" CAMPAIGN
2013

As a brand, Michelin is not only selling tires, but providing car lovers exceptional driving experiences.

The "Dream Driving" digital campaign, which aimed to engage Michelin’s premium audiences, rolled out across Weibo and key mobile platforms to inspire consumers to explore the ultimate driving experience and shared their dream driving route.

We invited social influencers to share their driving experience in extreme profession or daily life to inspire the community. Also, we designed a series of questionnaires to allow Michelin fans to explore their own extraordinary driving experiences across a number of landscapes, including Tibet, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Xi’an, Yunnan and Chengdu. Michelin established a full range of Chinese social platforms through this successful digital campaign.

I was responsible for the art direction and design. Michelle Wu was the copywriter. Dwayne Koh was the creative director.
