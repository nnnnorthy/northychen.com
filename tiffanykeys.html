TIFFANY KEYS CAMPAIGN
2015

Tiffany’s Keys is always about celebrating independent modern women who courageously embrace life challenges and dare to unlock the possibilities.

In 2015, we featured actor/director Sylvia Zhang and supermodel Liu Wen in their work environment, and fully immersed our audience deep into their thoughts and emotions. As the leading social content, we shot those two films to inspire the audiences. Also, it kicked off the topic and led to Tiffany's following digital campaign which spread on various social platforms.

My role on this campaign was the art director. Shanshan Quan was my copywriter partner. Vivian Yong was the creative director.
