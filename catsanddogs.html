CATS AND DOGS
2011

We took the idea is of being caught in a rainstorm and filtered it through the literal interpretation of the idiom - "It is raining cats and dogs." to give the user the experience of being inside of a cat and dog storm.

It took one afternoon for Felipe and I to make a conductive switch at the medal shop. Therefore, when someone opens the umbrella, the switch will turn on the cheap mp3 that we bought from the mall.
