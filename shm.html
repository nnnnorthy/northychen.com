NIKE SHANGHAI MARATHON CAMPAIGN
2016

There is only one way to see if a runner truly has the just-do-it spirit, you simply have to look at his/her sole. The wear, the tears, the scratches, the dirt, are the telltale signs of the runner's commitment, passion and unconditional love for running.

This campaign celebrated every runner’s unique commitment to running by visually focusing on the runner’s sole. We invited real runners to come to the shoot and asked them to share inspiring running stories. The outdoor prints rolled out massively in Shanghai during the marathon season, and truly impacted the whole city. Thousands of runners hold the soles up like badges of honour, wrote down their mantra and shared with the world.

I worked with Marula Vaz as art directors on this campaign. Shaun and Terence were our creative directors. Mike Tinney was one amazing photographer to work with.
