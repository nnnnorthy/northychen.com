NIKE SHANGHAI MARATHON CAMPAIGN
2016

There is only one way to see if a runner truly has the just-do-it spirit, you simply have to look at his/her sole. The wear, the tears, the scratches, the dirt, are the telltale signs of the runner's commitment, passion and unconditional love for running.

This campaign celebrates every runner’s unique commitment to running by visually focusing on the runner’s sole. We invite real runners to come to the shoot and share their inspiring running stories. The series of print roll out massively in Shanghai during the marathon season, and truly impact the whole city. Thousands of runners hold the soles up like badges of honour, write down their mantra and share with the world.

I work with Marula Vaz as art directors on this campaign. Shaun and Terence are the creative directors. Mike Tinney is one amazing photographer to work with.
