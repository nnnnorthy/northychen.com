Northy is an incurable romantic who believes that everyday objects around us also have feelings and “objectality”, just like human beings.

She builds up imaginary scenarios for everything around her:
a typewriter, a hamster, a sofa, a wine bottle & a glass. She once found a discarded massage chair, took it apart and reupholstered it. Later, she shared the plot she came up for the chair and collected 86 stories from narrators she picked up on the streets.

She currently lives in Shanghai and writes speculative stories at NERVEMILK.ORG.
