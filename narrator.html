NARRATOR
2012

This piece was the final charpter of my MFA thesis at RISD.
I took apart a used massage chair over the span of two weeks.
I documented the deconstruction of the chair and all the treasure I found inside it. The most surprising discovery was a pink earring, comfortably housed inside the seat cushion. I couldn't resist but wonder how it got there. I didn't only make up my own version of the story, but also asked strangers to make up theirs.

As a story collector, I met 86 narrators. They shared their take on the story in three different formats.
