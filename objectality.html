OBJECTALITY
2012

I coined the word "Objectality", to describe an object's personality. I illustrated short narratives for all of my personal belongings and divided them into different categories based on my interaction with them. I think that the interaction between humans and objects is like a consistent loop - full of imaginary stories.

I chose three objects I felt most attached to - a wall clock, a leather sofa and my electronic cords. I developed a short video prototype for each of them.
