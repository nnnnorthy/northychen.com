LITTLEBITS FOR MOMA STORES
2013

In 2013, I worked with crazy talented people at littleBits to attack MoMA stores in New York City. I was responsible to made this ferris wheel replica for the window display installation.
It was one of the demos at the MoMA design stores for the customers to interact with.

It was my first project for my wonderful time at littleBits. The final piece was the fifth Ferris Wheel I made, with blood, sweat and lots of useless rubber band.
