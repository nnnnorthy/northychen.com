FEDEX CITY CAPSULE
2016

I got the brief from Iain Tait when I was working at WK London Office. He asked if each Wieden office could curate one same size FedEx box with things to represent the city. We then got to ship them around the world to exchange various cultures.

I got to curate the Shanghai one, which included chicken feet, sandalwood soap, feiyue sneakers, a 80's wedding photo album inside a cigarette pack, dried fig snacks, haemorrhoids, golden throat lozenges and penis wine.

The above was a collection of pictorials I designed to introduce each object. The content included the product's basic intro, its famous advertisement, local mechanise, and a spread page of wallpaper.

I worked with Ben and Scott to initiate the concept and prototype the first round of package at the London office. Three months later, they shipped me a box packed with wonderful things, touching on London's economic issues, politics, food, drugs and more.
