HIT ME
2014

“Hit me.” The wine said.
So the glass did. Again and again.

I created this romantic story, and designed the mechanical serving stage for a wine bottle and a glass.

I embedded a weight sensor underneath the glass placement. The moving track inside the box was borrowed from a 3D printer. I also designed and transferred a series of graphics, showing different body parts' involvement during the activity of drinking wine.
