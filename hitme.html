HIT ME
2014

“Hit me.” The wine said.
So the class did. Again and again.

It says that drinking wine is a very sensual action. You watch the reflection on the glass, you shake it gracefully, you smell, you clink glasses and hear them chin-chin, and you sip the wine.

Therefore, I created this romantic story, and designed the mechanical serving stage for a wine bottle and a glass.
It was a toast to my beloved romance in 2014.

Beth and Casey from W+K Shanghai created the wine show called Autobottleography. "Hit Me" is one of the 39 wine bottles from their collection.
